Electrochemical Sensor Circuit Design

This page focuses on circuit-level design for electrochemical sensing systems, including low-current readout, transimpedance amplifiers, filtering, ADC interfacing, and analog front-end optimization.

My work includes nanoamp-level current detection, low-noise signal chain design, and PCB-based prototype implementation for sensor measurement systems.
